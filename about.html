About PetBuy Centre

Our Story

Founded in 2008, PetBuy Centre has been connecting loving families with healthy pets for over 15 years. What started as a small local pet shop has grown into a trusted adoption center serving the entire region.

Our Mission

We believe every pet deserves a loving home, and every family deserves the joy of pet companionship. Our mission is to make perfect matches that last a lifetime.

Our Services

Pet Adoption

We carefully select healthy pets from reputable breeders and rescue organizations. All pets undergo thorough health checks before adoption.

Training Programs

Our certified trainers offer obedience training, behavior modification, and specialized training programs tailored to your pet's needs.

Health & Wellness

We provide vaccinations, health checkups, and nutritional counseling to ensure your pet stays happy and healthy.
